NZ$1B AUA → ~A$7.1–9.5M annual recurring revenue at 71–95 bps. Operating leverage is structural; the acquirer's distribution channel sets the slope. Higher-AUA scenarios modelled in data room; not presented here because they depend on acquirer-specific distribution.
