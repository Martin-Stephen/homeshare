Why us

One scheme. Every deal.

The conventional single-asset structure for NZ commercial syndicates is a per-deal proportionate ownership scheme, limited partnership, or company. Each carries its own constitution, audit, AML programme, and investor register. Some syndicators have already moved beyond that model; if you have, Homeshare slots in beside what you’ve built. Where you haven’t, the comparison below holds.

About the founder

Martin van Blerk founded Homeshare in 2024. He previously founded Pixelmon, a digital collectibles project whose February 2022 primary mint generated approximately US$70 million in sales. The launch attracted critical public commentary, which the team addressed through a structured rebuild covering new leadership, a revised roadmap, and replaced art assets. Martin sold a controlling interest to LiquidX Ventures (Singapore) in October 2022, and Animoca Brands led a US$8.0 million seed round for the project in 2024.

Homeshare reflects two years of methodical product and regulatory work, applying the operating discipline of shipping at scale under public scrutiny to a fully-licensed New Zealand financial product.
