What you're buying.

Three working assets that take 24–36 months and A$3–4M to build from scratch.

A New Zealand–licensed scheme manager operating under the FMA's sandbox exemption (Financial Markets Conduct (Homeshare) Exemption Notice 2025). The exemption permits retail offers of fractional residential-property syndicates from low minimums, with a parallel pathway to full FMC Act MIS authorisation initiated.

A Master Trust Deed, drafted by MinterEllisonRuddWatts and executed in January 2026. The deed creates per-deal trusts — each property is held in its own ring-fenced trust, so the assets of one syndicate are unavailable to the liabilities of another. Trust-level tax efficient (PIE-elected), with no double-tax for the underlying retail investor. 36 clauses, full text in the data room.[A·8]

A deployed retail platform — production, staging, and development environments. Issuer admin console, retail front-end, KYC/AML pipeline, unit register, distribution engine, PIE reporting. Engineering team transferable. Architecture is acquirer-neutral: rebrand at the CSS layer.[A·9]

Why now.

Three reasons this is the right moment for a strategic acquirer.

Sandbox window

The FMA's exemption framework for retail-fractional property has a 12–18 month half-life before parity opens to the broader market. Homeshare is the only NZ retail-fractional scheme to have executed under it. First-mover regulatory positioning compounds: regulator relationships, deed precedent, and counterparty contracts cannot be expedited.

Precedent

Strategic acquirers buy distribution rails when they cannot build them at the speed required. Recent comparables: Praemium acquired the Iress OneVue Platform Business in 2024 (A$21M, $1M upfront + $20M earn-out, 18-month integration)[A·5]; Centuria acquired Augusta's distribution platform in 2020 (NZ$180M, 26% AUM uplift)[A·5]. Both deals were framed by the acquirer as buying a regulated distribution channel, not building one.

Founder transition

Founder is committed to a 60–90 day transition post-close with full handover of regulator, counsel, and engineering relationships. No earn-out required by the founder.

In plain English

FMA
Financial Markets Authority — the New Zealand securities regulator. Homeshare operates under an FMA-issued exemption notice that permits retail-fractional property offers.
Master Trust Deed
The constitutional document that creates and governs the Homeshare scheme. Executed January 2026 by MinterEllisonRuddWatts. 36 clauses. Reusable for every future deal.
Per-deal trust
A separate legal pocket within the scheme that holds one property and has its own investors and unit register. Each per-deal trust is ring-fenced from the others.
Ring-fenced
Assets of one per-deal trust cannot be claimed against liabilities of another (Deed cl 2.2). The structural protection retail investors require.
PIE election
Portfolio Investment Entity tax status — the per-deal trust pays tax at the investor's marginal rate (capped at 28%) and is tax-transparent. No double-tax for the underlying retail investor.
